Welcome to Sign2Text, an innovative app for recognizing sign language gestures in real time or from video, translating to your preferred language, and generating audio outputs.

About Sign Language

Sign language is a rich, expressive visual language using hand gestures, facial expressions, and body movements.
It empowers millions globally to communicate without spoken words and ensures inclusivity.

App Features

  • Real-time recognition via webcam or video uploads.
  • Translate detected signs into multiple languages instantly.
  • Generate audio output for English and the translated language.
  • View history of all detected signs and translations.
